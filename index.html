About LookFwd.

LookFwd is a mobile application that makes it easy to coordinate weekends with your friends. LookFwd allows users to simultaneously see their friends' real-time availability, message them and suggest plans to help bring friends together. LookFwd is the simple solution that makes it easy to spend more time with the people that matter most.

Goal: Bring People Together

The time you spend with the people that matter most can't be replaced. Yet, we find ourselves saying the following all to often:
- "I had no idea you were even in town!"
- "We'll have to get together on of these weekends when we're both free"
- "I would have texted you if I'd known you were going to be around!"
We miss countless opportunities everyday to spend time with the people that matter the most to us through a lack of transparency in our social schedules. If we simply had that real time information, we could be infinitely more efficient in making sure that we capitalize on those missed social opportunities and spend more, quality time with the people that matter the most to us. LookFwd provides that information, in an intuitive solution. Get ready to make more memories with your friends.

How to Use

Step 1: Slide when you're free this weekend and update anytime.
Step 2: Browse friends availability, and tap to message them.
